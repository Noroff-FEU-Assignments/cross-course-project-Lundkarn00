About us

Founded in 2011, Game hub is your ultimate destination for all things gaming. Whether you're searching for the latest releases, hunting for nostalgic classics, or looking to sell your own collection, we've got you covered. Our passion for gaming is what drove us to establish Game hub. As avid gamers ourselves, we understand the thrill and joy that comes with exploring virtual worlds, challenging friends, and experiencing the magic of interactive entertainment. Our goal is to provide a platform that caters to gamers of all ages and interests, ensuring that everyone can find their perfect game.

At Game hub, we believe in creating a seamless and secure marketplace for buying and selling video games. We have carefully curated an extensive collection of titles spanning various genres and platforms, from popular mainstream hits to indie gems waiting to be discovered. Our commitment to quality means that every game you find on our platform has been meticulously inspected to ensure authenticity and optimal condition.

The founders of Game hub, James Blake and Sebastian Capro, share a deep-rooted love for gaming. With a combined experience of over 20 years in the gaming industry, they bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the table. James has a background in sales, while Sebastian has a strong foundation in computer science. Their shared vision and dedication have been instrumental in shaping Game hub into what it is today. We understand that the gaming community thrives on connection and interaction. That's why we foster a vibrant community within Game hub, allowing gamers to engage with each other, share their experiences, and stay up-to-date with the latest news and trends. Join our forums, participate in discussions, and connect with fellow gamers who share your passion. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or just starting your gaming journey, Game hub is your one-stop destination for all your gaming needs. We are committed to providing exceptional service, a wide selection of games, and a seamless buying and selling experience.
Thank you for choosing Game hub. Let the games begin!
